Fix a Clogged Sink

Follow these steps to safely clear minor drain blockages.

Easy • 15–30 min

What You'll Need

Plunger Baking Soda Vinegar Hot Water
1

Remove Standing Water

Use a cup or bowl to remove excess water before beginning.

2

Use a Plunger

Seal the drain and pump firmly 10–15 times.

3

Apply Baking Soda & Vinegar

Pour ½ cup baking soda followed by ½ cup vinegar. Let sit 15 minutes.

4

Flush with Hot Water

Carefully flush the drain with hot (not boiling) water.

If the clog remains after these steps, submit a maintenance request.
